Travel and Tours Website

Travel & Tours Web Project – TheNaturesTrail.com
I independently designed and developed The Nature’s Trail, a travel and tour services website aimed at showcasing nature-based travel experiences. The project was built using WordPress as the main CMS, with custom enhancements through HTML and CSS to achieve a clean, responsive, and visually appealing layout. I was responsible for the entire website lifecycle — from gathering client requirements and wireframing to design customization, content integration, and performance optimization. In addition to development, I also provided full client support, handled feedback and revisions, and ensured the site met the client’s vision for a user-friendly and professional travel platform. The website features tour package listings, contact forms, blog integration, and a smooth navigation experience tailored for adventure and nature lovers.
