Then came internships - one after another, each adding something new. I learned how to work in teams, communicate clearly, and collaborate effectively. I got hands-on experience not just in development, but also in client support and project communication. I often handled direct client queries while contributing to project development. These experiences taught me the value of empathy, clarity, and responsibility in tech.

Over time, I realized I wanted to do more than just follow tasks. I wanted to build something real, meaningful, and scalable. Now, I’m in the phase where I’m continuously building, learning, and growing...actively looking for new challenges and opportunities to contribute to impactful work while learning from talented teams.
